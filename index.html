How this works

For each organic rank tier, weeks are regressed: purchase share % ~ PPC spend. The slope tells you how much extra purchase share an extra £1 of spend buys at that rank. That's converted to £ using total market purchases for the query and your margin, then compared to the £1 it costs — giving a true incremental profit verdict, not just a correlation.

This is an observational estimate from your weekly data, not a controlled experiment. Where possible, validate a "pause" verdict with a real on/off test before cutting spend entirely.
